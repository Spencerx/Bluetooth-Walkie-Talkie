Android Bluetooth Walkie Talkie

Description

The goal of this project is to make two mobile android devices function as walkie talkies. There are two assumptions that are made for the environment: the devices are already aware of each other (paired in Bluetooth) and they are within communication range. Communication will take place between the devices as push-to-talk.
